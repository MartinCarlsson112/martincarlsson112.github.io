The Game

Goblin Doctors is a top-down two player co-op game where you play as a pair of goblins who are tasked with saving injured orcs from a nearby battlefield. Unfortunately for the orcs, you are not exactly the best doctor in town. Your main way of "healing" their injuries tend to be to amputate and give them a prosthetic made from whatever scraps you can find. 

About the Project

Initially this project was developed over 8 weeks for the a game project at Campus Gotland by a team of 6. With four graphics artists, myself and another fellow programmer. The project is currently in redevelopment, make sure to keep posted!
