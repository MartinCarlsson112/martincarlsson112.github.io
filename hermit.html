The Game

The Hermit is a top-down adventure game where you play as the Hermit. Chased away from your home due to invading weasels you find yourself in a forest. You find that the weasels have also invaded this forest, but something else lives in this forest and their struggle is hard to ignore. With the help of these mysterious creature you must navigate through the forest and its ruins to prevent the weasels from destroying the forest.

About the project

The Hermit was developed in Unity over the course of 7 weeks.
